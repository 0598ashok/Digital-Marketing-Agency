# Digital Marketing Agency Website – Complete Template Blueprint

## Project Overview
**Template Type:** Service-Based Agency + Client Portal Dashboard  
**Industry:** Digital Marketing Agency  
**Dashboard Required:** Yes (Client Campaign Monitoring Portal)
