The PPC Sprint Methodology

How we design, launch, test, and optimize campaign segments.

Phase 1: Setup & Pixels

We deploy precise tracking configurations (Google Tag Manager, CAPI, Offline Conversion feeds) so ad metrics tie directly to sales outcomes.

Phase 2: Ads Deployment

Our content designers assemble creatives, and copywriters draft messaging arrays. Clients review and approve creatives via our portal prior to launch.

Phase 3: Bid Management

We continuously run A/B testing on audiences, copy variables, and adjust budget weights toward top-performing networks.

Paid Advertising FAQ

Are ad spends included in your monthly fees? ▼
No, monthly management fees cover campaign planning, asset production, code configurations, and bid management. Clients pay ad networks directly for media budget spent.
What is your process for approving creative assets? ▼
We post all creatives inside the dashboard Approval Center. You can review drafts, request adjustments, or click "Approve" with one click before campaigns go live.
